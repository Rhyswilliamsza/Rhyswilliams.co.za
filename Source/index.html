Hi,

My name is Rhys Williams, current student at Somerset College High School.

So, Who am I? Well, I grew up in Somerset West, Cape Town, and was lucky enough to attend Somerset House junior school, as well as Somerset College high school. I started my own company, RhysIT, in 2010 - and am currently running it as a small business. Whilst I have passions in video editing, filming, music making, photography, radio presenting and blogging, my main passion lies in IT. It has always come naturally to me, and hopefully one day, I may use it to change the world! If it's broken, I'll try fix it. If it's not broken - I'd probably break it, and then fix it.

You see, since my discovery of Lego way back in 2001, I have always had an interest in building. Not building in the sense of making a house, but rather in the sense of putting things together to make something new and unique. Whether through the total destruction and reconstruction of a piece of tech, or through a few lines of code, this trait still runs deep within me.

Ultimately, there's not much to say here. I guess the best way to get a sense of me would be to check out my blog or follow me on twitter. Send me a tweet - I'll be sure to respond :)
